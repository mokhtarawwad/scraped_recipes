Turkey Sandwiches With Maple Mayonnaise

Here’s a new fall classic for a young student’s lunch box: a fresh sandwich of turkey and apple, bound together with mayonnaise spiked with maple syrup. For a kick, swirl a little Sriracha sauce into the mayonnaise.

Featured in: Here’s Eating Lunch With You, Kid.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 ½ teaspoons maple syrup
  • ½ pound turkey or smoked turkey, sliced
  • 1 apple, thinly sliced

Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise and maple syrup; spread on 4 slices of bread, going all the way to the edges. Top with the turkey and apple slices and then with the remaining bread.

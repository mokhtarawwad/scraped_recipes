Applejack, Orange Peel

Ingredients

  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon simple syrup
  • 2 ¼ ounces Laird’s bonded applejack
  • Scant 1/2 ounce Demerara simple syrup
  • Dash of Fee Brothers Whiskey Barrel-aged bitters
  • Orange peel
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Build ingredients in old-fashioned glass, add ice, swirl and garnish with orange peel.

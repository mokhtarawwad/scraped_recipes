Cumin Rice

Pierre Franey

  • Yield 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons finely minced onion
  • 1 small clove garlic
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 ½ cups chicken broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Melt half of the butter in a saucepan and add the onion and garlic. Cook, stirring, until wilted and add the rice and cumin. Stir to coat.
  2. Add the chicken broth, salt, pepper and bay leaf. Cover and bring to the boil. Cook exactly 17 minutes. Uncover and stir in the remaining tablespoon of butter. Remove the bay leaf and serve.

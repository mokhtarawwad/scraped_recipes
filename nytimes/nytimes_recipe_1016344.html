This classic drink is essentially a ginned-up tall glass of lemonade.

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon simple syrup
  • 2 ounces London Dry-style gin
  • Soda water, to top
  • Lemon wheel or wedge, for garnish
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. In a cocktail shaker filled with ice, vigorously shake lemon juice, simple syrup and gin. Strain over fresh ice into a highball glass and top with soda water. Garnish with a wedge or wheel of lemon.

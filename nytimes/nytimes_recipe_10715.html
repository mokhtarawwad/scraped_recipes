Coleslaw

Christopher Idone

  • Yield Eight to 10 servings
  • Time1 hour 10 minutes

Vegetarian, Dairy Free, Nut Free, Mayonnaise, Red Cabbage, White Cabbage

Ingredients

  • 1 small head of white cabbage
  • 1 small head of red cabbage
  • 3 tablespoons salt
  • 1 cup homemade mayonnaise
  • 1 to 1 ½ cups vinaigrette
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • 1 red bell pepper, cored, seeded and cut into julienne strips
  • 1 teaspoon hot pepper flakes
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Discard the outer cabbage leaves and cut in half. Core, and beginning at the core end, slice thinly. You will have 15 to 16 cups.
  2. Place the cabbage in a large mixing bowl. Toss it with three tablespoons of salt and add cold water to cover. Let stand for one hour.
  3. Drain and dry thoroughly.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the mayonnaise with one cup of the vinaigrette and a half teaspoon of sugar.
  5. Add the cabbage and julienned pepper and toss with the dressing. Season with pepper flakes. If it is too dry, add additional vinaigrette.

Tip

  • can be prepared one day ahead.

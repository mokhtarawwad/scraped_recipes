Rhubarb Chutney

Barbara Kafka

  • Yield About 5 cups

Brown Sugar, Radish, Rhubarb

Ingredients

  • 8 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 4 medium shallots, cut into 1/4-inch dice
  • ⅓ cup peeled and diced ginger
  • 2 tablespoons Madras curry powder
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 ¼ pounds rhubarb stalks, trimmed and cut diagonally into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 15 medium radishes, halved and cut across into thin slices

Preparation

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat oil over medium heat. Stir in shallots, ginger and curry powder. Cook, stirring, for about 6 minutes, or until shallots are limp.
  2. Stir in sugar, rhubarb and radishes. Cover pot and raise the heat to medium-high. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 8 minutes, or until the rhubarb is tender but not mushy.

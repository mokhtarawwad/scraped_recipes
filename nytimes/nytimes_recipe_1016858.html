A friend who lived for a few years in Tel Aviv introduced me to this bright, easy highball. If you like a Greyhound, you'll like this, too. Personally, I think the lipsmacking licorice-y flavor makes it even more refreshing.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces grapefruit juice
  • 2 ounces arak
  • Seltzer, to taste
  • Grapefruit slice
  • Basil sprigs.
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice, and add the grapefruit juice and arak, and top with seltzer.
  2. Garnish with a thick grapefruit twist and a few sprigs of basil.

Eating Well Plum Torte

Marian Burros

  • Yield 8 servings

Vegetarian, Italian, Egg, Plum, Prune

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 4-ounce jar baby food prunes
  • 1 cup (plus 2 teaspoons) sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch salt
  • 12 Italian prune plums
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon, or to taste
  • Lemon juice
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Beat eggs, prunes and 1 cup of the sugar until blended.
  3. Beat in flour, baking powder and pinch of salt. Spoon into ungreased 8-, 9- or 10-inch spring form.
  4. Split and pit the plums, and place them on batter, skin side down, in concentric circles.
  5. Sprinkle generously with the cinnamon, 2 teaspoons of sugar and a few squeezes of lemon juice.
  6. Bake for about 40 minutes, until toothpick inserted near center comes out clean. Cool to lukewarm before serving. Can be reheated.

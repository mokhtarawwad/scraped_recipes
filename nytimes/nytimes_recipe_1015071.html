Camomile Flower

Ingredients

  • Camomile flowers
  • Water
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Per one quart of water, use one heaping tablespoon of camomile flowers.
  2. Steep 3 minutes for a mild brew, 10 minutes for a very strong brew. Delicious hot or iced.

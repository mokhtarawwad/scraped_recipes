Deltaville Shrimp Dip

Horacio Silva

  • Yield 8 to 10 servings

Gluten Free, Cream Cheese, Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 1 8-ounce package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 8-ounce can crushed pineapple, well drained
  • ¾ cup chopped cooked shrimp
  • ¼ cup finely chopped green bell pepper
  • ¼ cup finely chopped onion
  • ½ teaspoon seasoning salt or to taste
  • Crackers
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Make at least 2 hours ahead of serving so flavors will meld: combine all of the ingredients in a large bowl and mix well. Place in a serving dish, cover and refrigerate. Serve over some crackers.

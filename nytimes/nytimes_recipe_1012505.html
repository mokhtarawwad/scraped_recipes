Crab Salad With Cilantro, Tarragon and Grapefruit

Julia Moskin

  • Yield 6 servings
  • Time5 minutes

Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Cilantro, Lump Crab Meat

Ingredients

  • ½ pound lump crab meat
  • 2 tablespoons apricot kernel oil or almond oil or a neutral-tasting oil like grapeseed
  • ½ teaspoon sansho pepper (available at Japanese markets) or 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground green or black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon minced cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh tarragon
  • Freshly grated zest of 1/2 pink grapefruit
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, gently combine all ingredients. Taste and add more oil, pepper or grapefruit zest to bring flavors into balance. Keep chilled until ready to serve.

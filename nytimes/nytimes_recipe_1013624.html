Gluten Free, Brown Rice Flour, Corn Flour

4 12

Ingredients

  • 200 grams oat flour or very finely ground rolled oats (about 2 cups)
  • 250 grams brown rice flour (about 2 cups)
  • 210 grams sorghum flour (about 2 cups)
  • 40 grams corn flour or fine cornmeal (about 5 tablespoons)
  • 150 grams potato starch (about 1 cup)
  • 150 grams arrowroot (about 1 cup)
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Mix everything together and store in an airtight container.

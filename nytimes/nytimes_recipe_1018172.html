Egg, Nutmeg, Rum

4 15

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp. brown sugar
  • 2 oz. golden rum
  • Whole nutmeg
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Shake egg, sugar and rum thoroughly with cracked ice. Strain into a chilled wineglass. Dust with freshly grated nutmeg.

Za’atar-Spiced Zucchini

Za’atar, a Middle Eastern spice mix made from dried thyme, sumac, and sesame seeds, gives this grilled zucchini a fragrant and herbal twist.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds zucchini
  • ¼ cup za'atar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parsley, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Heat a charcoal or gas grill; the fire should be medium-low and the rack about 4 inches from the heat.
  2. Cut 1 1/2 pounds zucchini into 1/2-inch-thick slices.
  3. Stir 1/4 cup za’atar and 2 tablespoons olive oil into a paste; season with salt and pepper.
  4. Rub zucchini with za’atar and grill, turning once, until nicely browned and tender, 6 to 8 minutes. Garnish with parsley.

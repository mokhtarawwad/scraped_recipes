Louisville Hot Brown Sandwich

Marian Burros

  • Yield 4 servings

Southern, Bacon, Cheese, Turkey

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm cream sauce (use any standard recipe)
  • â…“ cup grated Cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices baked turkey, about 1/2 pound
  • 4 slices toast, crusts removed
  • 8 strips bacon, crisply cooked
  • 4 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Preheat broiler.
  2. Add grated Cheddar to cream sauce and stir to melt; keep warm.
  3. Place slices of turkey on the toast. Cover each with 1/4 cup sauce.
  4. Place 2 strips of bacon on each sandwich.
  5. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon Parmesan on top of each sandwich; place sandwiches on ovenproof dish and place under broiler
  6. Broil until bubbly and speckled brown.

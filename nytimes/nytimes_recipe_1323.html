Ham And Sweet Potato Hash

Barbara Kafka

  • Yield 4 - 6 servings
  • Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ pound cooked lean ham, cut into 1/4-inch dice
  • 1 pound sweet potatoes, cooked, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch dice
  • 1 small onion, peeled and cut in small dice
  • ½ medium green bell pepper, stemmed, seeded, deribbed and cut in small dice
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh sage or 1 teaspoon dried sage
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter

Preparation

  1. In a large bowl combine ham, potatoes, onion and green pepper. In a small bowl, whisk together cream, eggs, sage and pepper and add to the ham mixture.
  2. Preheat broiler. In a medium-sized skillet, melt 1 1/2 tablespoons of the butter over high heat. When almost brown, add the hash, reduce the heat to medium and cook for 4 minutes, gently shaking the pan occasionally to loosen the hash at the bottom.
  3. To brown the top, invert hash into an overproof dish and place under broiler for 3 to 5 minutes.

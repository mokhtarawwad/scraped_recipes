Smoked Lobster

Molly O'Neill

  • Yield Four servings
  • TimeAbout 30 minutes

Vegetarian, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Lobster

Ingredients

  • 4 1 1/2-pound lobsters
  • 1 tablespoon wood chips
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Steam the lobsters for 10 minutes. When cool enough to handle, remove the tail and claw meat. Place the wood chips in the bottom of a stove-top smoker and place the lobster meat on the rack over the chips. Place over medium heat until the wood begins to smolder. Cover and smoke for 10 minutes.

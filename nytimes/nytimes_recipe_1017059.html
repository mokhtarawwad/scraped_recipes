Sarah Anne Ward for The New York Times. Food stylist: Heather Meldrom. Prop stylist: Maeve Sheridan.

The aromas and flavors of corn, poblano chiles, grapes and citrus fruits harmonize in this elegantly spicy cocktail.

Featured in: Holy Smoke. 

Learn: How to Make Cocktails

5 10

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz. Vago Elote Mezcal
  • 1 oz. White Pineau des Charentes
  • ½ oz. Ancho Reyes liqueur
  • 2 dashes of orange bitters
  • Zest of a lemon.
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Stir the liquid ingredients together, pour over a chilled cocktail coupe and add lemon zest.

Braised Beets With Ham and Beer

Mark Bittman

Beer, Beet, Ham

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beets
  • greens
  • ½ cup chopped smoked ham hock
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ½ cup beer
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Peel beets, and chop them into 1-inch chunks; roughly chop greens. Put both in a saucepan with smoked ham hock or ham steak, butter and beer.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce to simmer. Cover, and cook until the beets are tender and the greens wilted, 20 to 25 minutes. Boil off any excess liquid. Garnish: Caraway seeds.

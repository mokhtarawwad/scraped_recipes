Savoy Cabbage

Bryan Miller And Pierre Franey

  • Yield 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 large savoy cabbage, cored, green leaves removed, and finely shredded (about 8 cups)

Preparation

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, add the cabbage, and stir. Return to a boil, reduce to simmer, and cook, stirring, for 3 minutes. Drain and chill.

Smothered Pork Chops With Tomato and Cream

Southern, Heavy Cream, Onion, Pork Chop

4 12

Ingredients

  • 4 large pork chops, at least 1 inch thick
  • Seasoned flour for dredging ( 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper per half-cup flour)
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 or 3 large Spanish onions, slivered
  • ¼ cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream (or more, as desired)
  • Salt and pepper as desired

Preparation

  1. Trim excess fat from the chops and dredge with flour, shaking off excess.
  2. Heat butter in a large, deep skillet and brown chops on both sides. Place onions on top of chops, cover skillet securely and cook over very low heat for 1 1/2 hours, or until very tender but not falling off the bone.
  3. Remove chops to a deep serving platter. Add tomato puree and cream to onions in skillet and boil, uncovered, until the mixture has a saucelike consistency. Season to taste with salt and pepper and serve with mashed potatoes.

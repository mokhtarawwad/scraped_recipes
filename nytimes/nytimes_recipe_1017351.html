Gin, Lemon Juice

5 6

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz. gin
  • ½ oz. fresh lemon juice
  • ½ oz. simple syrup
  • 2 dashes lemon bitters
  • 1 egg white
  • ¾ oz. Casoni 1814 (substitute Aperol if you can’t get your hands on this)
  • Crushed cardamom (a pepper mill works for this)
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Shake gin, lemon juice, simple syrup, lemon bitters and egg white in a shaker. Add ice, and shake for 10 to 15 seconds, until frothy. Strain into a coupe. Pour Casoni in center of cocktail (it will sink to the bottom). Sprinkle cardamom on top.

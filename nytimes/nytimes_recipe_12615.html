Vegetarian, Pepper, Red Wine Vinegar

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • ½ cup red-wine vinegar
  • olive oil
  • bay leaves
  • pinch of oregano
  • salt
  • peppers
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Bring 2 cups water, ½ cup red-wine vinegar, olive oil, bay leaves, a pinch of oregano and salt to a boil. Add peppers and cook for 1 minute; turn of heat, cover and let stand for 10 minutes. Drain before serving.

Wild Rice Pancakes

Marian Burros

  • Yield 4 servings
  • Time15 minutes

Vegetarian, All Purpose Flour, Wild Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups unbleached flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 cup cooked wild rice (about 1/4 cup uncooked)

Preparation

  1. Blend the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar.
  2. Stir in milk, eggs and melted butter just until ingredients are blended. Stir in cooked rice.
  3. Cook pancakes until they are brown on both sides. Serve with maple syrup.

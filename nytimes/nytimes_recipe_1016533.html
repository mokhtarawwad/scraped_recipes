This simple drink is a spin on the classic vodka-and-ginger-beer drink, the Moscow Mule. The combination of genepy, an herbal Alpine liqueur, the sweet snap of the Wondermint and the tangy bite of the ginger beer makes for a refreshing summer cocktail. The drink is named after a small town in Wisconsin where Mr. Bartels had his first restaurant job.

Featured in: Wisconsin Spirits, Meet New York Cocktails.

Ginger Beer, Lime Juice, Wondermint

5 10

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce Wondermint
  • 1 ounce Génépi liqueur, preferably Dolin
  • 2 ounces ginger beer
  • 1 bar spoon fresh lime juice
  • Piece of candied ginger, for garnish
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. In rocks glass filled with ice, combine all the liquid ingredients. Stir until chilled, about 30 seconds. Garnish with candied ginger.

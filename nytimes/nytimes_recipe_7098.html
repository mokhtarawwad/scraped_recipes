Horseradish Dumplings

Bryan Miller

  • Yield 8 to 12 dumplings

Jewish, Egg, Horseradish, Matzo Meal

Ingredients

  • 1 1-inch knob of horseradish, peeled and finely grated
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup matzoh meal
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons water

Preparation

  1. Steep grated horseradish in boiled water for five minutes. Strain, and let cool. In a bowl combine olive oil and eggs. Mix matzoh meal and salt together. Add matzoh meal to egg mixture. Mix in the horseradish and 2 tablespoons of water. Let stand for 20 minutes.
  2. Bring 2 quarts of salted water to a simmer. Using a tablespoon, form dumplings (about 8 large or 12 small) and poach for about 10 minutes (when they rise to the surface they need about a minute more cooking). Remove with a slotted spoon, and keep warm until serving.

Champagne, Lemon Juice, Pisco

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 red grapes
  • ½ ounce lemon juice
  • 1 ounce blended pisco (preferably La Diablada)
  • 3 ounces rosé Champagne

Preparation

  1. Make simple syrup: In a saucepan over low heat, warm sugar in 1 cup water until dissolved. Cool to room temperature before using. (There will be extra syrup; refrigerate if not using immediately.)
  2. In a mixing glass, muddle grapes. Add lemon juice, 1/2 ounce simple syrup and pisco, then shake with ice in a cocktail shaker.
  3. Strain into chilled flute. Top with Champagne.

Gin, Ginger, Orange Juice

5 19

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz. gin
  • ½ oz. fresh lemon juice
  • 1 small piece of fresh peeled ginger
  • ½ oz. simple syrup
  • 1 oz. orange juice
  • 2 dashes orange bitters
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Muddle the ginger in a mixing glass. Add ice and other ingredients. Shake. Serve in a Collins glass. Top with a splash of soda water. Garnish with an orange twist.

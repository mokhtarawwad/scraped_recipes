Roasted Garlic Jam

Molly O'Neill

  • Yield 2/3 cup
  • Time10 minutes

Garlic can be subtle and sweet, or it can be a big bully with acrid undertones. Its behavior depends on how it's cooked and in what company. Here, it cozies up with lemon juice, cayenne and parsley, blending fast into a zippy, soft topping for bruschetta, pizza or your favorite grilled meat.

Flat Leaf Parsley, Garlic, Lemon Juice, Olive Oil

Ingredients

  • 4 large heads garlic, roasted and peeled (see recipe)
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • Pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon coarsely chopped Italian parsley
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Chop the garlic with a knife until it forms a paste. Place in a bowl and stir in the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper and cayenne. Stir in the parsley. Use for bruschetta, pizza or grilled meats.

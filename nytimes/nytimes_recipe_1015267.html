Beet, Dijon Mustard, Dill

4 13

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beets
  • 1 minced shallot
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • ¼ cup sour cream
  • lemon juice to taste
  • chopped dill
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Peel and grate 1 pound beets.
  2. Make a dressing with 1 minced shallot, 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, 1/4 cup sour cream, lemon juice to taste and chopped dill.
  3. Garnish: Dill.

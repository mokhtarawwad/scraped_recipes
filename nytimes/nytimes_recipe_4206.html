Alsatian Mussels in Riesling

Florence Fabricant

  • Yield 6 appetizers or 4 servings as a main dish

Vegetarian, Nut Free, French, German, Mussel, Riesling

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 shallots, finely chopped
  • ½ bottle Alsatian riesling
  • 3 pounds medium-size mussels, scrubbed and debearded
  • 1 ½ tablespoons finely chopped parsley
  • 1 loaf crusty French bread
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Melt the butter in a heavy 6-quart saucepan. Add the shallots and saute over medium heat until they are tender but not brown.
  2. Add the wine and bring to a simmer. Add the mussels, cover and cook about 10 minutes, until the mussels have opened. Discard any mussels that do not open.
  3. Spoon the mussels into soup plates and spoon the broth over them. Sprinkle with parsley and serve with crusty bread.

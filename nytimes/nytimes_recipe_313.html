Vegetarian, Brussels Sprout, Mushroom

Ingredients

  • 1 quart brussels sprouts
  • 8 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced

Preparation

  1. Trim the brussels sprouts at the base, and using a sharp paring knife, cut an X into each base.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  3. Place the brussels sprouts in a heavy oven-proof casserole, and toss with 6 tablespoons of the butter, salt and a generous amount of pepper. Stir in the lemon juice.
  4. Cover tightly, and bake for 25 to 35 minutes, until the brussels sprouts are just tender. Baste and stir 2 to 3 times during the cooking.
  5. While the brussels sprouts are baking, heat the remaining 2 tablespoons butter in a large, heavy skillet. Saute the mushrooms over high heat until lightly browned, 5 to 8 minutes.
  6. When the brussels sprouts are done, mix in the mushrooms and serve.

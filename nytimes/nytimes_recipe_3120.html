Chocolate Chip Cookie Mix

Ingredients

  • 9 cups unbleached flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 3 cups firmly packed dark brown sugar
  • 3 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 cups vegetable shortening
  • 4 cups chopped pecans
  • 4 12-ounce packages semisweet real chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Combine flour, baking soda, salt and both sugars in very large bowl.
  2. Mix in shortening with fingers.
  3. Stir in pecans and chocolate chips. Store in airtight container in cool, dry place 6 months. For longer storage, refrigerate or freeze.

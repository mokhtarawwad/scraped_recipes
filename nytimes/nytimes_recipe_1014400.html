Cognac, Milk, Rum

5 14

Ingredients

For the Batter

  • 6 eggs (yolks and whites separated)
  • 1 pound sugar
  • 1 ounce añejo rum
  • 3 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • ¾ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground allspice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters

For Each Drink

  • 6 ounces milk
  • 1 ounce añejo rum
  • 1 ounce V.S. Cognac
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Make the batter: In a bowl, beat egg yolks until they are as thin as water. While beating, gradually add sugar, rum, vanilla, spices and bitters. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites until stiff. Fold into yolk mixture. If not using immediately, refrigerate batter.
  2. Make the drink: In a small saucepan, bring milk to boil. Meanwhile, warm a roughly 10-ounce Irish coffee mug in the oven. Pour in 2 ounces batter. Add 1 ounce rum and 1 ounce Cognac. Fill with boiling milk, stirring briskly with a small whisk while adding, so batter and milk are well mixed. Dust with nutmeg.

Tip

  • Because batter contains raw eggs, it should be refrigerated when not being used, at no higher than 41 degrees. Batter should be used only on the day it is made, then discarded.

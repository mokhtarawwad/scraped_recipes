Risotto With Spinach And Pine Nuts (Ristorante La Brisa)

Ingredients

  • ⅛ cup pine nuts
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • 4 cups water
  • ¾ pound spinach, washed, ends trimmed
  • 1 ½ tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped onions
  • 1 cup Arborio rice (see note)
  • ¼ cup Parmesan cheese or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Place pine nuts in one layer in oven-proof dish, and place in oven for 15 minutes until they become golden. Remove and cool to room temperature.
  2. In another pot, heat 4 cups water and cook spinach until limp. Remove, drain, squeeze dry and chop finely.
  3. In a pot, heat chicken stock to boiling, lower heat and keep at gently rolling boil.
  4. In a frying pan, heat 1 1/2 table spoons butter and 1 tablespoon oil. Add onions and saute until translucent. Add rice and saute for 1 to 2 minutes, stirring until rice is warm to the touch. Add half-cup of boiling chicken stock, stirring constantly until rice absorbs stock. Add 2 more half-cups of stock in half-cup amounts, again stirring while stock is absorbed. (Liquid should be bubblinggently.) Add spinach at this point, when rice is half-cooked. Stir and mix.
  5. Add rest of stock, in half-cup amounts (total cooking time of rice is approximately 30 minutes). Five minutes before rice is cooked, add Parmesan and 2 tablespoons of butter, and mix. Just before serving, mix in all but 1 tablespoon of the pine nuts, sprinkling the top with remaining nuts. If desired, add more Parmesan to taste.

Tip

  • Arborio rice can be found at Italian markets and specialty stores.

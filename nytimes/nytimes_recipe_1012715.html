Summer Tomato Gratin

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ripe, firm tomatoes, sliced
  • Salt
  • freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • ½ cup fresh or dry bread crumbs, preferably whole wheat
  • 2 tablespoons chopped flat-leaf parsley
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Oil a 2-quart gratin or baking dish. Layer the tomatoes in the dish, seasoning each layer with salt, pepper and a small sprinkle of sugar.
  2. Toss together the bread crumbs, parsley and olive oil. Spread over the tomatoes in an even layer. Place in the oven, and bake for 1 to 1 1/2 hours, until the juices are thick and syrupy and the top is golden. Remove from the oven, and allow to cool for at least 15 minutes before serving.

Tip

  • Advance preparation: You can make this dish several hours ahead. It is excellent served at room temperature. Martha Rose Shulman can be reached at martha-rose-shulman.com.

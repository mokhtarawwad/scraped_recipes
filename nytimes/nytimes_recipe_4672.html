Zucchini au Gratin

Pierre Franey

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-size zucchini, about 1 1/4 pounds total
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup white onions, sliced thin
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic
  • 2 teaspoons finely chopped fresh rosemary leaves, or 1 teaspoon dried
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
  • 4 tablespoons Parmesan or Gruyere cheese

Preparation

  1. Preheat broiler.
  2. Trim off the ends of the zucchini and cut them crosswise into thin slices. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  3. Put olive oil in a large nonstick skillet over high heat. Add zucchini and onions. Cook, tossing or turning with a spatula, until lightly browned, about 3 minutes. Add garlic, rosemary and parsley and cook 1 minute more. This may have to be done in two steps if the skillet is not large enough.
  4. Transfer zucchini into a big, shallow baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese. Place under broiler about 4 inches from heat source, until lightly browned.

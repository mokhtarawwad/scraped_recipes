Bitters, Bourbon, Champagne

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce Baker’s bourbon
  • ½ ounce Cointreau
  • 4 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 4 dashes Peychaud’s bitters
  • Chilled Champagne
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Fill pint glass with ice. Add all ingredients except Champagne and stir briskly until chilled.
  2. Strain into chilled flute and top with Champagne.

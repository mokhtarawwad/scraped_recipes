Marian Burros's Onion Confit

Marian Burros

  • Yield Enough for 6 servings of beef

Ingredients

  • 3 large white onions
  • 2 tablespoons peanut oil
  • ⅓ bunch fresh sage leaves, chopped
  • 2 ounces champagne vinegar

Preparation

  1. Peel onions, and slice into thin strips.
  2. Heat peanut oil in a large heavy skillet and saute onions over medium heat, stirring often. After about 30 minutes of cooking it might be necessary to add a little water to prevent them from sticking.
  3. After 1 hour add the sage leaves and vinegar and continue cooking, stirring often, 30 minutes more. Set aside until beef is done.

Tip

  • The confit can be made a day ahead and refrigerated. Warm slowly to serve.

Grilled Lime Tuna

Marian Burros

  • Yield 2 servings
  • TimeAbout 30 minutes

Lime, Tuna

Ingredients

  • 1 thick slice of tuna 10 ounces
  • ½ lime
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Preheat broiler if using.
  2. Wash and dry tuna, and squeeze juice from lime over both sides. Let tuna sit no more than 20 minutes.
  3. Prepare stove-top grill if using. Measure tuna at thickest point, and cook eight minutes to the inch, grilling or broiling on both sides.

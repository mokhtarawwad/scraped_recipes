Three-Herb Iced Tea

This nonalcoholic iced tea is made with fresh mint, basil and a simple syrup infused with chamomile flowers. It is as cool and refreshing as an air-conditioned subway car after a long wait on the steamy platform.

Ingredients

  • 8 sprigs fresh mint
  • 4 large leaves fresh basil
  • Chamomile syrup, to taste (see recipe)
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Bring 3 cups of water to a boil. In a glass measuring cup or teapot, pour water over mint and basil. Let cool, then strain into ice-filled glasses. Sweeten with syrup to taste.

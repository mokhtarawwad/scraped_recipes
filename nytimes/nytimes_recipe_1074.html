Greek Cauliflower Dip

  • Yield Four cups

Ingredients

  • 2 large heads cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 6 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 3 cups chicken broth, homemade or low-sodium canned
  • 3 ounces feta cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon freshly ground pepper, plus more to taste

Preparation

  1. Place the cauliflower, garlic and chicken broth in a deep saute pan. Bring to a boil. Reduce to a simmer. Cover and cook until , about 50 minutes. Drain.
  2. Place the cauliflower and garlic in a food processor. Puree until smooth. Add remaining ingredients. Process until smooth. Serve with crackers.

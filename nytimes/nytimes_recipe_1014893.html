Watermelon Daiquiri

Ingredients

  • 1 small watermelon
  • ⅓ cup lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • ½ cup rum
  • some ice
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Peel 1 small watermelon (about 3 pounds), and cut the flesh into large chunks. Purée them in a blender with 1/3 cup lime juice (or more to taste), 1 tablespoon honey, 1/2 cup rum, and some ice until cold and smooth. Serve in chilled glasses.

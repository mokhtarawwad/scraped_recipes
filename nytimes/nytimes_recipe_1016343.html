Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 ounces London Dry-style gin
  • Soda water, to top
  • Lime wedge, for garnish
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice and add the gin. Top with soda water and garnish with a wedge of lime.

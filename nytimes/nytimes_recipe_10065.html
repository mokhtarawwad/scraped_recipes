Sauteed Spinach With Pine Nuts

Pierre Franey

  • Yield 4 servings

Olive Oil, Pine Nut, Spinach

Ingredients

  • ¾ pound fresh spinach
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 4 tablespoons pine nuts
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Pick over the spinach leaves and remove the large stems. Wash and drain well. Remove all the moisture by placing between paper towels and patting dry.
  2. In a large skillet over high heat, heat 1 tablespoon of the olive oil and half the spinach, half the nutmeg and half the pine nuts. Add salt and pepper to taste. Cook and toss very quickly until the spinach is just wilted. Drain. Repeat the procedure with the remaining ingredients and serve hot.

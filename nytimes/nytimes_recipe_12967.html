Vegetarian, Nut Free, Indian, Ginger, Mint, Yogurt

5 26

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups medium-thick plain yogurt
  • ½ teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon roughly chopped mint leaves
  • 1 teaspoon finely chopped green serrano or jalapeño chile
  • Pinch cayenne
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the yogurt, ginger, mint, chile and cayenne. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Let flavors meld at least 5 minutes. (May be prepared several hours ahead.) Serve chilled.

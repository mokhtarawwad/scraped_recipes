Brown Sugar, Heavy Cream

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup white sugar

Preparation

  1. In a small heavy-bottomed saucepan mix all the ingredients and bring to a boil over medium heat. Cook, stirring almost constantly, until reduced and thickened, about 20 minutes. This is delicious not only on this pudding but also on just about everything except steak.

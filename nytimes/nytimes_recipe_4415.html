Spaghetti With Parmesan Cheese

Pierre Franey

  • Yield 4 servings
  • Time15 minutes

Vegetarian, Italian, Basil, Linguine, Parmesan

Ingredients

  • ¾ pound thin linguine (linguini fini)
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup finely shredded fresh basil leaves
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ½ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese, plus additional cheese to be served on the side
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Bring about 3 quarts water to a boil and add linguine, stirring down so the pasta is completely immersed in the water. Add salt and cook until tender, stirring strands often, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain.
  2. Return pasta to the kettle it was cooked in, and add butter, olive oil, basil, pepper and 1/2 cup cheese, and toss to blend. Serve with additional Parmesan cheese on the side.

Davide Luciano for The New York Times. Food stylist: Maggie Ruggiero. Prop stylist: Gozde Eker.

The name and color of this cocktail may bring to mind sunlight playing on the East River (or, if you prefer, the arguably more romantic Gowanus Canal). Whatever it evokes, it’s delicious — a bold but balanced blend of botanical flavors from floral gin, gentian liqueur and Aperol.

Featured in: Flowers In Your Glass. 

More +

Aperol, Edible Flower, Gentian Liqueur, Gin

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz. gin
  • ½ oz. gentian liqueur
  • ½ oz. Aperol
  • ½ oz. fresh lime juice
  • Edible flowers to garnish
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a coupe. Garnish with an orange twist and flowers.

The Firestones' Brandy- Snap Cups With Mousse Fillings

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey

  • Yield Fourteen servings
  • Time10 minutes

Raw, Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 14 brandy-snap cups (see recipe) (see note)
  • 6 cups dessert mousse
  • 14 tablespoons sweetened whipped cream
  • 14 candied violets, pieces of candied ginger or another sweet garnish
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Place one brandy-snap cup on a dessert plate.
  2. Spoon half a cup or less of dessert mousse inside the cup, such as pumpkin, praline (see recipe) or orange juice with Grand Marnier (see recipe).
  3. Spoon one tablespoon of whipped cream on top and place one candied violet or other sweet garnish on top.

Tip

  • You may, of course, reduce the number of brandy-snap cups used, adjusting the remaining ingredients accordingly.

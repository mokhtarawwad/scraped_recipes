Southwestern, Lime Juice, Tequila, Tomato Juice

Ingredients

  • 3 serrano chilis
  • 12 ounces gold tequila
  • 46 ounces tomato juice
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 ounces freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 lime

Preparation

  1. Finely chop the serranos and allow them to macerate in the tequila for 10 minutes. Finely chop the cilantro and mix all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker or pitcher with ice. Pour through a strainer into wine or martini glasses and garnish each with a slice of lime.

Tip

  • The chilis and other Southwestern ingredients can be ordered from Los Chileros de Nuevo Mexico, P.O. Box 6215, Sante Fe, N.M. 87502.

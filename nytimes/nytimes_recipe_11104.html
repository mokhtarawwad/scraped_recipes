Broiled Tomatoes

Pierre Franey

  • Yield 6 servings
  • TimeAbout 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 medium red ripe tomatoes
  • 24 slivers garlic
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Preheat the broiler to high, 500 degrees.
  2. Cut the tomatoes in half and arrange, cut side up, in a baking dish. Insert 2 garlic slivers in each half.
  3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and olive oil. Sprinkle with the cheese.
  4. Place under the broiler for about 5 minutes until bubbling and the garlic tips start to brown. Remove garlic, sprinkle with basil and serve.

Maria Cenname, a bartender at the Black Horse Pub, specializes in a variant of the bloody mary, which features an orange-flavored vodka.

Featured in: Maria's Bloody Mary. 

More +

Clamato Juice, Vodka

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz. Stolichnaya orange
  • 3 oz. Clamato juice
  • ½ tsp. soy sauce
  • juice of 1 lemon wedge
  • ½ tbsp. horseradish
  • 4 dashes powdered ginger
  • 4 dashes hot sauce
  • a pinch of pepper
  • pinch of salt
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Shake with ice, garnish with carrot sticks and a slice of cucumber.

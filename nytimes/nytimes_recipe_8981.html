Fettuccine With Tomato And Lovage Sauce

Barbara Kafka

  • Yield 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fettuccine or spinach fettuccine
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, cut into 1/4-inch dice (about 3/4 cup)
  • 4 medium cremini mushrooms, cleaned, trimmed, cut in half and then lengthwise into 1/4-inch-wide slices
  • 2 medium cloves garlic, smashed, peeled and minced
  • 3 medium tomatoes, cut into 1/4-inch dice (2 cups)
  • Â¼ cup lovage leaves, cut crosswise into thin strips
  • Kosher salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta, and cook for 8 to 12 minutes, until done.
  2. Heat the oil in a medium-large frying pan over medium heat. Add the onions, and stir to coat in the oil. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 2 minutes. Add the mushrooms and garlic. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 minutes. Add the tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes, or until the tomatoes have collapsed. Stir in the lovage, and cook for 2 more minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Drain the pasta, and add it to the frying pan with a little of the pasta cooking liquid. Toss to coat, and cook for 1 or 2 minutes.

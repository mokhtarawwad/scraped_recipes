Chipotle Powder, Orange Juice, Tomato

Ingredients

  • 3 medium-size ripe tomatoes
  • ¾ teaspoon chipotle powder, or to taste
  • Juice of 1 1/2 oranges
  • Juice of 1 1/2 limes
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Halve and core tomatoes, place cut-side up in a baking dish with a half-inch of water, dust with chipotle powder and roast 1 hour. Allow to cool.
  2. Peel tomatoes, chop and mix in a blender with citrus juices. Season to taste with salt and, if desired, more chipotle. Chill.

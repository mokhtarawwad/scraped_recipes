Espresso, Heavy Cream, Sugar

Ingredients

  • ½ cup sugar
  • 4 tablespoons water
  • 3 tablespoons hot espresso
  • ½ cup heavy cream

Preparation

  1. Dissolve the sugar in the water in a heavy saucepan. Place over medium-high heat, and cook, swirling occasionally, until the mixture becomes a medium amber color. Remove from the heat.
  2. Pour the hot espresso into the caramel, and stir to blend. Add the cream, stirring to blend completely. Refrigerate; serve with cake, ice cream and drained poached fruit.

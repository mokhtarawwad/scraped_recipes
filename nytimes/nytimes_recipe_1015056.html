Ingredients

  • 1 pound zucchini
  • Â¼ cup olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Parmesan, shaved, to taste
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Peel 1 pound zucchini into ribbons with a vegetable peeler; spread them out on a platter.
  2. Whisk together 1/4 cup olive oil and the juice of one lemon.
  3. Drizzle over zucchini ribbons; season with salt and pepper, and scatter shaved Parmesan over the top.

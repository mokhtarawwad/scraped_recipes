Brownie Ice Cream Sundaes

  • Yield 8 servings

The ice cream sundae has shed its youthful, frivolous shell. This is a balanced, thoughtfully constructed dessert, something that nourishes the intellect and the palate, not just the pangs of the stomach.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Brownies (see recipe)
  • Cherry sauce (see recipe)
  • Fudge sauce (see recipe)
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. In an electric mixer fitted with whisk attachment, whip cream at medium-high speed until it begins to thicken. Add sugar and vanilla, and continue to whip until cream forms soft peaks.
  2. In an ice cream dish, surround three scoops of ice cream with 4 to 6 brownie cubes. Top with a little cherry sauce, a generous spoon of fudge sauce and a big dollop of whipped cream. Serve.

Raw Pesto Scallops

Creamy, sweet, briny and meaty at the same time, scallops are among the most user-friendly of mollusks, and this recipe, which comes together in 30 minutes or less, won’t unnecessarily complicate things. Buying good scallops is more than half the battle. Treating them simply once you get them to the kitchen is the rest.

Ingredients

  • 6 large scallops
  • 1 cup basil
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ½ small garlic clove
  • ¼ cup Parmesan
  • salt and pepper

garnish

  • Toasted pine nuts (optional)

Preparation

  1. Slice 6 large scallops into thin rounds and put them on a plate.
  2. Put 1 cup basil in a food processor with 1/4 cup olive oil, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 1/2 small garlic clove, 1/4 cup Parmesan, salt and pepper.
  3. Process, stopping to scrape the sides of the bowl, until smooth.
  4. Spread the pesto over the scallops. Garnish: Toasted pine nuts (optional).

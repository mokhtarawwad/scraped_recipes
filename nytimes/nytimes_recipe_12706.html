Vegetarian, Vegan, American, Basil, Corn, Tomato

5 48

Ingredients

  • Raw corn kernels
  • Sliced cherry tomatoes or chopped plum tomatoes
  • Lots of basil
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice or good vinegar

Garnish

  • More basil
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Combine raw corn kernels with sliced cherry tomatoes or chopped plum tomatoes and lots of basil in a large bowl.
  2. Add olive oil and lemon juice or good vinegar; toss.
  3. Garnish: More basil.

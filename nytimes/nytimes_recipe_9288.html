Grilled Pork Chops With Blueberry Salsa

Ingredients

The salsa:

  • 2 cups fresh blueberries, cleaned
  • ½ medium red onion, peeled and diced small
  • 2 jalapenos, seeded and minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, cored, deribbed and diced
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • ¼ cup fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt

The pork:

  • 4 pork chops, 3/4-inch thick
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. To make the salsa, coarsely chop 1 1/2 cups of the blueberries. In a bowl, combine the chopped and whole berries and the remaining salsa ingredients. Let stand for 1 hour.
  2. To make the pork, prepare a charcoal grill. When the coals are hot, grill the chops until just cooked through, about 8 minutes per side. Season with salt and pepper. Divide among 4 plates and top with the salsa. Serve immediately.

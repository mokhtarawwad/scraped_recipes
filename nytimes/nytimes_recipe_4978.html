Strawberry Preparado

Elaine Louie

  • Yield 1 serving

Raw, Mexican, Milk, Strawberry, Sugar

Ingredients

  • ½ pint fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced (about 1 cup)
  • 1 cup milk or ice water
  • 4 ice cubes
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons sugar, or to taste
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a blender, and process until smoothly puréed.

Gin, Juniper Berry, Tonic Water

Ingredients

  • 3 juniper berries
  • 1 lime wheel
  • 1 lemon peel
  • 1 leaf lemon verbena
  • 1 ½ ounces Hendricks gin
  • 1-6 8/10-ounce bottle Fever-Tree tonic

Preparation

  1. To a wide-rimmed rocks glass or stemless red-wine glass, add juniper berries, lime wheel, lemon peel, lemon verbena and a large ice cube. Add gin. Slowly add tonic water to taste.

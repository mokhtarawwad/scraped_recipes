Spinach Bites

Molly O'Neill

  • Yield Thirty-six spinach balls
  • Time30 minutes

Vegetarian, Nut Free, Bread, Egg, Spinach

Ingredients

  • 2 packages of frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 2 cups Pepperidge Farm stuffing
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • ½ cup melted butter plus butter for the pan
  • ½ cup grated parmesan
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Form into bite-size balls and place on a lightly buttered cookie sheet. Bake for 20 minutes and serve.

Tip

  • The spinach balls can be frozen on the cookie sheet. Defrost 20 minutes before baking and then bake as above.

Baked Pears With Saba

Florence Fabricant

  • Yield 6 to 8 servings

Vegetarian, Italian, Bosc Pear, Lemon, Saba

Ingredients

  • 6 bosc pears, peeled, cored and halved
  • Juice of 2 large lemons, and grated zest of one
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 ¾ cups saba or vin cot
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees. Toss pears in a bowl with lemon juice. Butter a 10- or 11-inch round or 9-by-13-inch baking dish. Arrange pears in dish. Sprinkle with lemon zest and cinnamon.
  2. Bake about 1 hour, basting every 20 minutes with 1/3 cup saba. Remove from oven. If pan juices are runny, spoon into a saucepan, and cook down until syrupy.
  3. Serve pears warm or cool, with pan juices and remaining saba drizzled over them.

Veal Steak With Pesto (Hilaire)

Marian Burros

  • Yield 4 servings
  • Time15 minutes

British, Cherry Tomato, Pesto, Veal

Ingredients

  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 4 veal steaks from the loin, about 5 ounces each
  • 24 cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Pesto (see recipe)
  • 2 teaspoons finely sliced green onion tops
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Season the veal steaks with salt and pepper and grill on both sides until pink, about 5 minutes altogether.
  2. Saute tomatoes in butter over high heat until they are ready to pop, about 2 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, spread a layer of pesto on top of each steak until all the pesto is used. Run steaks under broiler, until pesto is bubbly.
  4. Place each steak in middle of plate with juices. Arrange cherry tomatoes around steak, spoon juices into plate. Sprinkle with green onion tops.

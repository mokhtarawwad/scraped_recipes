Ingredients

  • 1 cup cucumber juice (see recipe)
  • 2 tablespoons plain lowfat yogurt
  • 4 teaspoons finely chopped scallions
  • 2 teaspoons finely chopped fresh basil
  • 1 medium clove garlic, peeled and minced
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Whisk the cucumber juice and yogurt together in a medium bowl. Whisk in the scallions, basil, garlic, salt and pepper.

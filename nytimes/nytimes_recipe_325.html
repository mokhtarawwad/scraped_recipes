Ginger Braised Brussels Sprouts

Marian Burros

  • Yield 8 servings

Low Calorie, Brussels Sprout, Ginger

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • ¾ cup chicken stock
  • 40 Brussels sprouts, washed and trimmed
  • 1 ½ pounds onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tablespoons grated fresh ginger, coarsely chopped
  • ¼ teaspoon hot-pepper flakes (optional)
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. In a pot large enough to hold all the ingredients, melt the butter in the chicken stock.
  2. Reduce heat, and add the sprouts, onion, ginger and hot-pepper flakes. Cover and simmer about 20 minutes, until sprouts are tender but still crisp and bright green.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, and serve.

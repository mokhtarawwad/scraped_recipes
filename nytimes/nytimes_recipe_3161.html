Eastern European, Bacon, Mushroom

Ingredients

  • 1 pound mushrooms
  • 6 slices bacon
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1 cup water
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream

Preparation

  1. Wash the mushrooms, and cut into thin slices.
  2. Dice the bacon, and fry until lightly browned in a 12-inch skillet.
  3. Dice the onion, and add to the bacon.
  4. Fry the mixture, stirring, until the onion is just wilted. Add the mushrooms.
  5. Over low heat, stir with a spoon for 10 minutes.
  6. Add flour, water and salt.
  7. Bring water to boil, and boil for 5 minutes until the mixture has the consistency of gravy.
  8. Add the sour cream. Stir until well blended.

Lemony Chickpea-and-Tuna Spread

Pierre Franey

  • Yield About 2 cups

Low Cholesterol, Canned Tuna, Chickpea, Sesame Tahini

Ingredients

  • 1 15-ounce can of chickpeas, drained
  • ⅓ cup sesame paste (tahini), stirred well
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled
  • 2 scallions, chopped
  • ¼ cup water
  • ½ teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh coriander
  • ⅓ cup canned water-packed tuna, drained
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Tabasco to taste
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in the bowl of a food processor. Puree to a coarse texture. Cover and keep cool. Serve on croutons, crackers or bread.

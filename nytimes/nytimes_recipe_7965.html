Steamed Fava Beans With Thyme

Moira Hodgson

  • Yield 4 servings

Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Fava Bean

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds fava beans (in their pods)
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Remove the pods from the fava beans. If you like, peel their skins.
  2. Put beans in top of a steamer and sprinkle them with thyme. Cook them until they are tender (this depends on their size and whether they are peeled, but it shouldn't take longer than 15 minutes).
  3. Put the beans in a warm serving bowl, add the butter, salt and pepper and toss them. Serve immediately.

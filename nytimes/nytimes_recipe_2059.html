Toast With New York State Goat Cheese

Marian Burros

  • Yield Ten servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cylinder fresh New York State goat cheese, 6 ounces
  • 1 teaspoons fresh thyme, coarsely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon fresh rosemary, coarsely chopped
  • Coarsely ground black pepper to taste
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons Cognac
  • 10 slices country bread

Preparation

  1. Cut the cheese into half-inch slices.
  2. Mix the thyme, rosemary, pepper, oil and Cognac together and marinate the cheese slices in the mixture for four hours, or longer if desired.
  3. Slice a loaf of country bread, about the diameter of a baguette, into 10 slices. Brush the marinade from the cheese on both sides of the bread and toast.
  4. Arrange a slice of cheese on the toast and place in a 300-degree oven, toasting for four to five minutes, until the cheese is hot. Serve hot.

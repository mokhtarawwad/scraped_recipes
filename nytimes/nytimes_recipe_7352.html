Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Garlic, New Potato, Yogurt

Ingredients

  • 1 pound tiny new potatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 4 tablespoons nonfat yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons light sour cream
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Scrub potatoes but do not peel. Peel garlic. Place potatoes and garlic in pot with enough water to cover, then cook in a covered pot until potatoes are tender.
  2. Drain and mash potatoes and garlic. Stir in yogurt, sour cream, salt and pepper, and serve.

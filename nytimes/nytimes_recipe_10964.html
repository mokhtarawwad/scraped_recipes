Marshmallow

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces wafers (in any color you like) for candy coating (see note)
  • 48 marshmallows
  • 1 28-inch lollipop sticks
  • Plastic foam block or another support for the sticks
  • Aluminum foil
  • Wax paper
  • 1 cup or more edible decorations (crushed nonpareils, jimmies, sequins, sprinkles)
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Simmer candy wafers in the top of a double boiler, stirring occasionally (do not let them stiffen). Thread 4 marshmallows on each stick. Cover a square of plastic foam with aluminum foil.
  2. Hold a marshmallow stick over the pan and spoon the melted coating over it, covering it more or less completely. Hold over a piece of wax paper and sprinkle with edible decorations -- the zanier and more colorful the better. Insert the stick into the foam. Repeat with remaining marshmallows. Refrigerate until coating hardens.

Tip

  • Candy-coating wafers are available at New York Cake and Baking Distributors, (212) 675-2253.

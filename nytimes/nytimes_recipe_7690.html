Tomatoes With Basil And Mozzarella

Marian Burros

  • Yield 3 servings
  • Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ pound ripe yellow tomatoes or red tomatoes with vines attached
  • ¼ pound fresh mozzarella
  • 9 large basil leaves (more if leaves are small)
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Wash and slice tops and bottoms of tomatoes; cut tomatoes into about three slices each.
  2. Slice the mozzarella very thinly; wash and dry the basil leaves.
  3. Arrange the mozzarella and basil on the tomato slices on three salad plates,and sprinkle with pepper.

Mizuna With Sweet Vinaigrette

Marian Burros

  • Yield 2 servings
  • Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces mizuna
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon honey mustard

Preparation

  1. Trim, wash and dry mizuna; cut into small pieces.
  2. Whisk oil, vinegar and mustard in serving bowl; stir in greens, and mix well.

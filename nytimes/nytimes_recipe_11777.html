Carrot Vinaigrette With Rosemary And Walnut Oil

Vegetarian, Vegan, Carrot Juice, Walnut Oil

Ingredients

  • 1 cup carrot juice (see recipe)
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh rosemary
  • 2 teaspoons walnut oil
  • ½ teaspoon salt, plus more to taste
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Place the carrot juice in a medium-size bowl. Whisk in the lemon juice, rosemary, walnut oil, salt and pepper. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.

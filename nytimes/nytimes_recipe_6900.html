Dungeness Crab Toasts

Marian Burros

  • Yield Eight servings
  • Time10 minutes

American, Crab Meat, Firm White Bread, Mayonnaise, Scallion

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Dungeness crab meat
  • ¼ cup scallions, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • Salt to taste
  • Cayenne pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice, or to taste
  • 8 slices good-quality white bread, crusts removed, each cut in 1/2 on the diagonal
  • 8 sprigs of chervil, for garnish
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. In a mixing bowl combine the crab, scallions and mayonnaise. Season to taste with salt, cayenne and lemon juice.
  2. Toast the bread slices and spread with the crab mixture. Garnish with a small sprig of chervil and serve immediately.

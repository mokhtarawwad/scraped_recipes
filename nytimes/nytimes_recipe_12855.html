Cointreau, Lime, Tequila

5 17

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. Herradura reposado tequila
  • 1 oz. Cointreau
  • the juice of 1 medium lime

Preparation

  1. Shake ingredients with ice and serve on the rocks in a tall glass.

Pomegranate Syrup, Rum

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon pomegranate syrup or molasses
  • 2 ounces Montecristo rum (or other dark rum)
  • 2 ounces orange juice

Preparation

  1. Pour the ingredients into a shaker filled partly with ice, shake and pour the contents, unstrained, into a rocks glass. Garnish with pomegranate seeds (optional).

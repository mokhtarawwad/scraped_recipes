Pear And Stilton Sandwiches

Liz Logan

  • Yield Two sandwiches
  • Time15 minutes

Vegetarian, Bread, Pear, Stilton

Ingredients

  • 4 very thin slices honey-oat bread
  • 1 tablespoon butter, softened
  • 1 ripe pear, halved and thinly sliced
  • Lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled Stilton cheese (about 1/2 ounce)
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Spread each bread slice with the softened butter. Sprinkle the pear slices with the lemon juice. Place half of the pear slices in a single layer on a slice of the bread. Top with half of the crumbled cheese and a second bread slice. Repeat, using the remaining pear slices, cheese and bread.

Minted Yogurt Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 cup best-quality whole milk yogurt
  • ½ cup crème fraîche
  • ¼ cup mint, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chives, minced
  • ½ clove garlic, finely diced or grated on Microplane
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Tabasco or other hot pepper sauce, to taste
  • Kosher salt
  • freshly ground black pepper, to taste
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Combine yogurt, crème fraîche, mint, chives, garlic and lemon zest in a medium-size nonreactive bowl, then whisk to a smooth consistency.
  2. Season to taste, transfer to small bowls and serve with lamb ribs.

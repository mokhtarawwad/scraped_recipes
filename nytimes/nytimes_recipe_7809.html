This recipe for the classic bourbon (sometimes brandy) and ginger ale highball came to us from the Campbell Apartment, a cocktail lounge in Grand Central Station.

Featured in: Shaken And Stirred; Redder And Bluer. 

More +
4 9

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces bourbon, rye whiskey or brandy
  • 3 dashes bitters
  • About 6 ounces ginger ale
  • Orange twist
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Pour liquor into an 11-ounce highball glass filled with ice. Add bitters and top off with ginger ale. Lightly stir and garnish with orange twist.

Warm Shrimp and Beans

Florence Fabricant

  • Yield 4 servings

Ingredients

  • Â½ pound dried cannellini beans
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 7 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 20 jumbo shrimp, shelled and deveined
  • 2 large garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary leaves
  • 1 cup diced ripe tomatoes
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Cover beans with cold water to a depth of 2 inches above the beans. Soak for 4 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain the beans and place in a saucepan. Cover with cold water to a depth of 2 inches above the beans, bring to a boil, skim the surface, then lower the heat to medium. Cook the beans until they are tender, about 45 minutes. Drain, reserving 1/3 cup of the cooking liquid. Season the beans with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Have four plates ready. Heat 2 tablespoons of the oil in a large skillet, add the shrimp and stir in the oil until golden on both sides, approximately 2 to 3 minutes. Add the garlic, rosemary, tomatoes, beans, reserved 1/3 cup of liquid from the beans and salt and pepper to taste. Cook, stirring gently for 2 minutes. Remove from heat and add remaining oil.
  4. Divide among the plates, arranging five shrimp on each like spokes of a wheel and mounding the bean mixture in the center. Serve.

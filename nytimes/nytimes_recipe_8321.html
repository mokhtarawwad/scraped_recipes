Monterey County Jail Oatmeal

  • Yield 1 divided steel plateful
  • Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • A lot of powdered oatmeal
  • Way too much water
  • All the sugar the county will let you have
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Boil the water and stir in the oatmeal. Let steep.
  2. Sprinkle or deluge with sugar, to taste.
  3. Eat with a steel spoon.

Sauteed Piquillos

Mark Bittman

  • Yield 4 appetizer servings

Vegetarian, Spanish, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Garlic, Piquillo Pepper

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • 2 teaspoons peeled and slivered garlic
  • 8 to 12 piquillo peppers, cut into strips
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Sherry vinegar (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place olive oil in a large skillet, turn heat to medium-low, and add garlic. Cook, shaking pan occasionally, until garlic turns light brown, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add peppers, and turn heat to medium. Cook, shaking pan, until peppers just begin to change color on bottom; turn, and repeat. Season to taste, and serve hot or at room temperature, drizzled, if you like, with a little more olive oil and sherry vinegar.

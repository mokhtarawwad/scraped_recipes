Ginger, brandy, and a slice of peach make for a simple, graceful cocktail.

Featured in: Jack And Ginger, All Grown Up. 

More +

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce ginger liqueur (such as Domaine de Canton)
  • 2 ounces Cognac (nothing too fancy)
  • ½ ounce fresh lemon juice
  • 1 slice of peach
  • 1 slice pickled ginger

Preparation

  1. Stir ginger liqueur, Cognac and lemon juice with ice in a mixing glass.
  2. Strain into a chilled coupe.
  3. Impale peach slice and pickled ginger together.

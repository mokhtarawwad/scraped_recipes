French, Heavy Cream, Potato

Ingredients

  • 1 pound round white potatoes, peeled and sliced thin
  • Â½ cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Arrange the potatoes in layers in a buttered 9-inch baking dish, pouring on a little cream and adding butter, salt and pepper to each layer as you go.
  3. Top with cream, salt, and pepper and bake for 30 to 40 minutes, or until the potatoes are cooked and the top is brown.

Honey, Peanut Butter, Sweetened Condensed Milk

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces creamy peanut butter
  • 6 ounces condensed milk
  • 6 ounces honey
  • 3 ounces water

Preparation

  1. Combine peanut butter with milk and honey and blend, adding water until creamy.

Hellmann's is a fine product. But homemade mayonnaise is something entirely different. It is perfectly delicious spread on sandwiches, but it can also become the foundation of a more complex dipping sauce or dressing. Make sure you use a mildly flavored oil so the richness of the egg, as fresh as you can find, can shine.

Egg Yolk, Oil

Ingredients

  • 1 egg yolk
  • Sliver of a garlic clove, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon or English mustard
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • Sea salt
  • 1 cup peanut oil or vegetable oil
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together egg yolk, garlic, mustard, lemon juice and a large pinch salt. Beginning a drop at a time, whisk in oil. As it emulsifies, add oil a little faster, in a slow steady stream. When mixture is thick, taste and adjust seasoning. It should be highly seasoned.

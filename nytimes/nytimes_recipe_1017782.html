The vintage-cocktail expert Ted Haigh considers allspice dram an ideal “secret ingredient”: A small amount can add tremendous depth and flavor to many classic drinks. Here, I use it to perk up the otherwise staid — if indisputably delicious — Brandy Alexander.

Brandy, Cream

5 5

Ingredients

  • 1 oz. brandy
  • ¾ oz. crème de cacao
  • ¼ oz. allspice dram
  • 1 oz. heavy cream
  • Whole nutmeg
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Shake brandy, crème de cacao, allspice dram and cream vigorously with ice. Strain into a cocktail glass. Grate a very small amount of nutmeg on top.

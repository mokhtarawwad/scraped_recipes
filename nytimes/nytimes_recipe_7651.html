Creme De Cassis, Gin, Grapefruit Juice

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce gin
  • 1 ounce Plymouth Sloe Gin liqueur
  • ½ ounce crème de cassis
  • ¾ ounce fresh grapefruit juice
  • ½ ounce fresh lemon juice
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Combine the ingredients and shake. Strain into a chilled glass and serve.

Peach Cobbler

  • Yield Eight to ten servings

Butter, Peach, Self Rising Flour

5 162

Ingredients

  • 8 ripe peaches
  • 12 tablespoons butter
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 cup plus two tablespoons self-rising flour
  • ½ cup milk

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Peel, pit and slice the peaches. Melt 4 tablespoons of butter in a medium saucepan. Add the sliced peaches and 1/2 cup sugar. Stir in 2 tablespoons of flour and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes, until soft and syrupy.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine the remaining cup of sugar with the remaining cup of flour. Stir in the milk.
  4. Melt 8 tablespoons of butter in a 9-inch-by-13-inch baking pan. Pour in the batter, reserving about 1/3 cup.
  5. Pour the peach mixture over the top of the batter. Drizzle the reserved batter over peaches, and spread around evenly to cover.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven for about 1 hour, or until top is golden brown. Serve warm. (Top with ice cream, if desired.)

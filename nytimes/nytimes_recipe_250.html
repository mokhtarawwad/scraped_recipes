Broiled Oysters With Salsa

Amanda Hesser

Anaheim Chili, Oyster, Tomato

Ingredients

  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • Rock salt
  • ¾ cup finely diced fresh Anaheim chilies
  • 1 ½ tablespoons minced white onion
  • ½ teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro, plus extra for garnish
  • Salt
  • 24 oysters, opened and on the half shell
  • ⅓ cup sour cream

Preparation

  1. Bring medium pot of water to a boil, and blanch tomatoes for 8 seconds. Rinse under cool water, and remove skins. Halve tomatoes, discarding pulp and seeds, and cut into 1/8-inch dice; there should be about 3/4 cup.
  2. Line a serving platter with a layer of rock salt, and set aside.
  3. In medium bowl, combine tomato, chilies, onion, garlic and 2 tablespoons cilantro. Season with salt to taste.
  4. Preheat broiler with rack 6 inches under heating element. Place oysters in broiling pan, and broil 1 minute. Transfer to prepared platter. Top each oyster with small spoonful of salsa, and dab of sour cream. Garnish platter with sprinkling of cilantro, and serve immediately.

St. Louis Deep-Fried Raviolis

Marian Burros

  • Yield 8 to 10 servings
  • Time22 minutes

Milk, Parmesan, Ravioli

Ingredients

  • 50 uncooked raviolis, fresh or frozen
  • 1 ½ cups milk or evaporated milk
  • 1 ½ cups dry bread crumbs
  • Corn oil for deep frying
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Meat, tomato or butter sauce
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. If raviolis are frozen, defrost. Dip raviolis into milk and then into bread crumbs.
  2. Heat the oil to 375 degrees and deep-fry the raviolis until golden brown, two to three minutes; turn to cook evenly. Remove from the oil; drain.
  3. Sprinkle with cheese and serve, with sauce for dipping.

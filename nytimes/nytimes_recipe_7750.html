Fortified Wine, Vodka

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Ciroc Vodka
  • 1 ounce Pineau des Charentes
  • Dash of Grand Marnier
  • 1 gooseberry
  • Twist of lime
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Stir the vodka and the Pineau des Charentes together in a mixing glass, with ice, until chilled.
  2. Strain into a cocktail glass that has been rinsed with Grand Marnier. Garnish with gooseberry and lime twist (squeezed to release its oil)

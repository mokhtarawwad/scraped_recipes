French, Cognac, Lemon Syrup, Lillet Blanc

4 9

Ingredients

  • 1 grapefruit segment
  • 3 dashes Fee Brothers Grapefruit Bitters
  • ½ ounces Morris Kitchen Preserved Lemon Syrup
  • 2 ounces Lillet Blanc
  • 1 ounce cognac
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. In a mixing glass, muddle the grapefruit with the bitters.
  2. Fill with ice, add the other ingredients and shake vigorously.
  3. Strain into a chilled coupe
  4. Garnish with a grapefruit twist

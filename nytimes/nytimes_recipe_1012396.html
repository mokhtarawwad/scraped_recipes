Spanish, Cava, Dry Spanish Sherry, Vermouth

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce dry Spanish sherry, such as fino, amontillado or oloroso
  • 1 ounce sweet vermouth
  • 2 ounces cava (or dry Champagne or prosecco)
  • 1 lemon peel for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Add sherry and vermouth. Stir rapidly until outside of shaker is frosted, then strain into a chilled martini glass. Top with cava. Garnish with lemon peel.

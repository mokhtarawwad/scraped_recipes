Blue Cheese, Chive, Garlic, Mayonnaise, Milk, Sour Cream

5 6

Ingredients

  • ¼ pound blue cheese, crumbled, about two-thirds cup
  • ½ teaspoon finely minced garlic
  • ¼ cup finely chopped chives
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • ¼ cup milk or water

Preparation

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl and blend well.

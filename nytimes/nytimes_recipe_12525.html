Gin, Green Chartreuse, Maraschino Liqueur

5 15

Ingredients

  • ¾ ounce gin
  • ¾ ounce maraschino liqueur
  • ¾ ounce green Chartreuse
  • ¾ ounce lime juice
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Shake all ingredients with ice. Strain into a chilled coupe glass.

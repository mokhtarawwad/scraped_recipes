Southern, Mustard Green, Pork Shoulder, Turnip Green

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds cured pork shoulder
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
  • ¾ pound turnip greens, well rinsed
  • ¾ pound mustard greens, well rinsed
  • 1 small onion, peeled and sliced
  • ¼ cup apple cider vinegar

Preparation

  1. Combine pork and 6 cups water in a large saucepan. Bring to a boil and simmer gently over medium-low heat until it reduces to 1 quart, about 2 hours. Remove pork and discard. Season broth with pepper.
  2. Add the greens and cook over medium heat until tender, stirring frequently, about 15 minutes. Using a slotted spoon, transfer the greens to a large glass or ceramic bowl. Reserve the broth for the dumplings (see recipe). Add the onion and vinegar, toss to combine. Serve immediately.

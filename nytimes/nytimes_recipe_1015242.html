Scotch, Vermouth

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ ounces Sheep Dip blended scotch whisky
  • 1 ¼ ounces sweet vermouth
  • ⅓ ounce Benedictine
  • ¼ ounce absinthe (or Pernod)

Preparation

  1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass. Add ice and stir for 50 revolutions.
  2. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Twist the lemon peel over the surface to release oils, then use as garnish. Optional: Serve with shortbread cookies on the side.

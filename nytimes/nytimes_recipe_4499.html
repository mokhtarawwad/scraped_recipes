Raw, Balsamic Vinegar, Olive Oil, Red Pepper Juice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red pepper juice (see recipe)
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Place the pepper juice, vinegar, lemon juice, olive oil, salt and pepper in a food processor and process for 30 seconds.

Thrice-Cooked

Ingredients

  • slab of bacon
  • chopped onion
  • carrot
  • celery
  • red wine
  • Thyme or parsley
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Brown a slab of bacon on all sides; remove with a slotted spoon. Add chopped onion, carrot and celery, and cook until onion is soft. Add red wine, and boil out a bit; add stock and bacon; cover and simmer until very tender. Remove the bacon, pat dry and sear it in a skillet or on the grill. Slice and serve with reduced braising liquid. Garnish: Thyme or parsley.

Bourbon, Milk

4 22

Ingredients

  • 1 quart whole milk
  • 1 cup half-and-half
  • 1 ½ cups bourbon, other whiskey or brandy
  • 1 cup powdered sugar, sifted
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • Freshly grated nutmeg, for garnish

Preparation

  1. In a pitcher, combine milk, half-and-half, bourbon, sugar and vanilla and stir. Place in freezer until slushy, at least 3 to 4 hours or up to 24 hours. Stir well and serve in chilled glasses, sprinkled with nutmeg.

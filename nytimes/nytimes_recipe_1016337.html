Updated Whiskey-Ginger Highball

A dressed up version of an easy favorite, galvanized by fresh ginger and orange.

Featured in: Jack And Ginger, All Grown Up. 

More +
5 6

Ingredients

  • 2 slices raw, peeled ginger (about the size of a quarter)
  • 1 bar spoon Morris Kitchen ginger syrup
  • Ginger beer (I like Gosling’s)
  • 3 ounces rye
  • Orange peel twist to garnish
Email Grocery List

Preparation

  1. Muddle one slice of ginger with syrup and a bit of ginger beer in the bottom of a highball glass. Fill glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add rye and stir with a bar spoon. Add ginger beer to fill.
  3. Run the other slice of ginger around the rim of the glass, then discard. Twist orange peel over drink to release oils, then drop into glass.
